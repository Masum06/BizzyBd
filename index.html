Intro

You have a great story to tell, and you want to tell it to the rest of the world. But, not in a place like Facebook where your story will be drowned into selfies and baby photos, not in Twitter where we can speak no more than 140 characters. No. None of them represents who you are. You deserve a place of your own, to tell your story, who you are, what you care about. And there is no place in the world to tell it, better than a dot com after your name.

In March 15, 2017 BizzyBd.com is going to introduce one of the easiest tool to make a website IN THE WORLD. So that you can tell your story, in your way, in your place, in matter of minutes.

How to submit
At Bizzy, we are constantly challenging the web-developing technology and redefining what people can do with it. We think working at Bizzy will be a lifelong adventure for you. We are searching for some web-designers who will be just as passionate as we are about changing people's lives with technology. That's why, your application doesn't require any CV, CGPA or Educational Institution. Just solve at least one of the following questions, and send them to apply@bizzybd.com.

Shortlisted applicants will be called for an interview.

Deadline: February 10, 2017


Go to: How to submit

Solve The Four Problems:

Stage 1

If you are told to redesign Facebook timeline page, so that it is more beautiful, and user-friendly, how would you do it?

Hint: You can submit an HTML page, Photoshop, Illustrator, Word or paint design of how your version of Facebook look like. Even hand drawn picture is allowed. Only your sense of design is evaluated in this question.

Stage 2

Make a webpage as cool, or cooler than this: http://www.apple.com/iphone-se/ One more thing, only using HTML, CSS and Bootstrap.

Hint: In this stage we are trying to understand your skill of HTML, CSS and Bootstrap. We will see how close to this page you can go.

Stage 3

Design the front page of a blogging website. Make it as colorful, dynamic, beautiful you can. There is no restriction to use any template, framework. Put some animation, gradient, shadows if you think appropriate. Use whatever you want, and make a stunning blogging page.

Hint: In this stage we try to understand how good you can utilize the resources available online, and make a beautiful webpage.

Stage 4

Surprise us! :)
How to submit:
  • Make a folder with your email address as the foldername (i.e. yourname@email.com).
  • For each question you attempt, make a subfolder (i.e. 'Stage1', 'Stage2').
  • Put all necessary files inside corresponding stage folders
  • Zip the folder, and send it to apply@bizzybd.com as an attachment with your name, or
  • Upload the main folder to Git, Dropbox, GoogleDrive, or anywhere you like, and email the link to apply@bizzybd.com.
  • It's not mandatory to solve every Stage.
  • If you don't understand any question, you are free to make any assumption.
